Inventory & Sales Tracking System

System Overview

The system is designed to digitize inventory management and sales tracking, replacing manual record keeping with a centralized and efficient solution.

Purpose

To improve accuracy in stock control, reduce human error, and provide real-time visibility of inventory, sales, and item movement within an organization.

Key Features

• Inventory management
• Sales tracking
• Category & supplier management
• Reporting and analytics
• User access control

Benefits

• Faster decision making
• Reduced stock loss
• Improved operational efficiency
• Better data accuracy
